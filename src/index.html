How to play:

Click 'Play Melody' to play a small tune! Your goal is to try to copy that melody, note by note, by playing it back on the piano! Every melody you successfully copy will get you points. The harder the melody, the more points you get! You only have a certain amount of time to copy a melody per tune. Successfully copying a melody will rest the timer. From the 'Settings' tab, you can change your scale, key, or difficulty. These changes will apply once you restart the game. Choosing a harder difficulty will your game harder, but you will also gain a score multiplier. Some scales can also earn you more points, depending on how many flats are in the scale.
